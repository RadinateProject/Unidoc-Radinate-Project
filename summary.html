Radinate: Post‑Market AI Validation Platform

Ensuring radiology AI stays safe and effective — continuous local testing, drift detection, and regulatory readiness.

The Problem

The radiology supply–demand gap is vast. AI in medical imaging can transform care, but models that look great in the lab can fail in local, real‑world settings. Without local testing and continuous monitoring, AI-driven decisions risk automation bias and may harm patient outcomes.

Our Solution

Radinate is a post-market AI validation platform designed to fill this gap. We enable hospitals to conduct local testing, identify AI model limitations, and continuously monitor for performance drift. By providing confidence intervals on AI’s decisions, we bridge the gap between clinicians and AI — building trust and supporting FDA regulatory compliance.

Key Features

AI Drift Detection & Re‑validation

Identify errors in real time, reducing manual review by up to 80%.

Regulatory Compliance

PCCP and EU AI Act‑ready reports for smoother approvals.

Vendor Collaboration

Built‑in feedback loops improve vendor models and accelerate regulatory approvals.

Confidence Intervals

Deliver statistically sound confidence on model decisions to reduce automation bias.

Why Now?

The FDA and EU are mandating continuous post‑market monitoring, which becomes critical by 2026. Radinate helps hospitals adopt AI safely and remain compliant.

Market Opportunity

AI in imaging is expected to grow to $4.54B by 2029. Radinate positions to capture an estimated $55M annual revenue within five years.
